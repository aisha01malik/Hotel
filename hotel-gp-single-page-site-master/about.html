About us

We’re passionate about offering you the finest of Indian hospitality and a holiday experience.

Located in the tranquil farmlands near Jaipur, in a living orchard surrounded by graceful, green plantations, Resortica aims to transform the weary soul through the wellness of body, mind and soul.


It is not just a retreat that will calm you with the enchantment of its surroundings, but also offers a relaxing and luxurious stay in a quiet and natural location withinall the amenities. 

True wellness is more than the mere absence of disease; it is the presence of life-changing forces that fill our lives with vitality and meaning. Wellness is an omnipotent word and it means many things to many people; to us Wellness simply is a transformative experience that rejuvenates body, mind and soul.
